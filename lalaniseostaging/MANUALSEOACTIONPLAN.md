# Manual SEO Action Plan

This document separates automated, code-level tasks from tasks that require manual human intervention, business outreach, and verification. 

## Automated vs. Manual Task Breakdown
- **Automated/Code Tasks (Handled by Developers):** Schema JSON injection, `robots.ts` updates, meta tags, lazy-loading optimizations, and alt text definitions.
- **Manual Tasks (Detailed Below):** Account verifications, content gathering, client outreach, and local directory submissions.

---

## Tasks that Must Be Done Manually

### 1. Google Business Profile Verification & Optimization
- **Action:** Claim and verify the Google Business Profile (GBP) for Lalani Computers at "59, Janmabhoomi Marg".
- **Action:** Upload high-resolution, professional photos of the office, warehouse operations, and team members.
- **Action:** Ensure the business category is accurately set to "Computer store" or "IT service management company".
- **Action:** Ensure business hours, phone number, and website URL match the `lalanischema.json` data exactly.

### 2. Local Citation Submissions (Mumbai & India)
- **Action:** Submit the business to local Indian directories (e.g., Justdial, Sulekha, IndiaMart, TradeIndia).
- **Action:** Audit existing citations to ensure strict NAP (Name, Address, Phone Number) consistency across the web.

### 3. Partner/Vendor Backlink Outreach
- **Action:** Reach out to brand partners (HP, Dell, Lenovo, Cisco, Microsoft).
- **Action:** Request to be listed on their official "Authorized Resellers", "Partner Locator", or "Where to Buy" pages. These provide highly authoritative, industry-relevant backlinks.

### 4. Real Testimonial Collection & Review Management
- **Action:** Contact existing enterprise clients (e.g., Tata Motors, IDBI Bank, Kores India) and request detailed reviews on your Google Business Profile or LinkedIn company page.
- **Action:** Assign a team member to respond to *every* review (both positive and negative) on Google to signal active management.

### 5. Case Study Data Gathering
- **Action:** Interview internal project managers to gather specific, quantifiable metrics for case studies (e.g., "Reduced server room cooling costs by 30%", "Deployed 500 workstations across 3 branches in 14 days").
- **Action:** Provide this raw data to the content team to replace the placeholders in the newly generated landing pages (e.g., `datacenterlandingpage.html`).

### 6. Business Directory & Niche Submissions
- **Action:** Submit the site to B2B IT service directories and industry-specific portals (e.g., BFSI tech vendor lists, Healthcare IT procurement portals).
